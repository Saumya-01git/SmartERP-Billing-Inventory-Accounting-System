# Requirements

## Main Users
- Business Owner
- Accountant
- Operator

## Core Modules
- Authentication
- Company Management
- Ledger Management
- Customer Management
- Supplier Management
- Stock Item Management
- Purchase Voucher
- Sales Voucher
- Invoice PDF
- Reports

## Application Flow
1. User registers or logs in
2. User creates/selects company
3. User creates customers and suppliers
4. User creates stock items
5. User records purchase
6. Stock quantity increases
7. User records sale
8. Stock quantity decreases
9. Invoice PDF is generated
10. Reports are shown
